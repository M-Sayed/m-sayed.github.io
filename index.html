About Me

I'm a Senior Software Engineer with over 7 years of experience building robust backend systems and leading technical initiatives. Currently at Accredible, I develop backend systems which serve millions of users and process millions of records. My expertise spans from building real-time analytics dashboards to migrating applications to modern cloud infrastructure (eg: Elasticbeanstalk to ECS)

As a vetted member of Toptal's exclusive network of top 3% software engineers, I have delivered high-impact solutions for leading technology companies including Zeus Living, Corsair Gaming, and Owens Corning. Through these engagements, I've enhanced GraphQL APIs, optimized critical endpoints, and implemented robust authentication systems while maintaining the highest standards of code quality and testing.

My journey began with competitive programming, where I qualified for the ICPC World Finals and earned various medals and placements. This foundation in algorithms and problem-solving continues to drive my approach to engineering challenges.

I specialize in performance optimization, distributed systems, and DevOps practices and initiatives. From reducing page load times by 10x to architecting real-time analytics dashboards, I'm passionate about creating efficient, scalable solutions.
